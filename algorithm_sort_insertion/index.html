插入排序

  • ​ 伪代码：

    • INSERTION-SORT(A)

for j=2 to A.length
  key=A[j]
  //Insert A[j] into the sorted sequence A[1..j-1]
  i=j-1
  while i> and A[i]>key
    A[i+1]=A[i]
    i=i-1
  A[i+1]=key
